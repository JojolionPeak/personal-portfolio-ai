Software Engineer

My journey into tech began during my sophomore year of college when I stumbled across a Python tutorial and became fascinated by the idea of building things from nothing but logic and code. What started as weekend curiosity quickly turned into a passion, leading me to shift my academic focus toward computer science and web development. Over the past few years I have worked on full-stack projects that deepened my understanding of both frontend design and backend architecture. Today I am driven by a desire to build meaningful, user-centered applications and continue growing as an engineer.

About Me

I have always been drawn to the intersection of creativity and logic, which is why web development feels like such a natural fit for me. On the technical side, I am especially interested in building clean, responsive user interfaces and designing efficient RESTful APIs. Outside of coding I enjoy hiking on weekends and exploring local trails whenever I get the chance. I am also an avid reader, particularly of science fiction novels that explore the future of technology and humanity. Music is another big part of my life — I play guitar in my spare time and love discovering new artists across different genres.
